About Us

All you need to know about CEVS enterprenuer Oduduwa university

Our school entrepreneurship program empowers students to explore and develop their entrepreneurial skills through a range of specialized courses tailored to their interests. Students can choose from various programs, including Web-development, barbing, hair-dressing, Event planning, and more. Each course is designed to provide hands-on learning, critical thinking, and problem-solving experiences, preparing students to turn their ideas into successful ventures. With the guidance of experienced mentors, students will gain the confidence and knowledge needed to navigate the business world and become future leaders and innovators.
